How do you raise a three-year old?

Emily Oster, Professor of Economics and International and Public Affairs, Brown University

The author of Cribsheet and Expecting Better joins us for a conversation about how data can help us become better, more relaxed parents.

Amy O'Hara, Research Professor, Georgetown Massive Data Institute

A conversation about the messy intersection of data governance, evidence building, and privacy in pursuit of leveraging our administrative data for the public good.
